Evacuation Simulation for a High-Speed Railway Station During Construction
Built a BIM-integrated evacuation model in Pathfinder to simulate mixed evacuation of 2,400 workers and passengers during the construction phase.
Evaluated emergency scenarios (e.g., stair blockage and guided evacuation) to identify bottlenecks and flow inefficiencies.
Reduced total evacuation time by 23.7 seconds and improved efficiency by 10.16%, supporting data-driven safety planning and layout optimization.
